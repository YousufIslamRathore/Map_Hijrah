Residency Permit Services, Anywhere in Turkey

Here's what we'll do for you:
  • Complete the application resulting in an appointment date, time, and place, and send your application document to you within 24 hours.
  • Advise and assist you in collecting all required documents.
  • Advise and assist you in obtaining any foreign documents and apostilles.
  • Advise and assist you in obtaining Turkish sworn translations and notarization of foreign documents.
  • Provide the required Turkish health insurance policy for the duration of your residence permit, within 24 hours.
Conditions
  • Before you apply, you must enter Turkey first, with a visa or in visa-exempt status.
  • You should get a Turkish mobile phone with a Turkish mobile telephone number to recieve messages from the immigration office.
  • Your passport must be valid for 60 days beyond the duration of the residence permit you want.
  • For Istanbul applications (and sometimes other provinces), the immigration office may ask for additional documents, including documents from your home country, such as a criminal record.
